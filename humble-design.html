Imagine going from nothing to having a furnished home! Humble Design furnishes houses top to bottom for homeless in shelters going into housing and veterans. They provide everything to make a house a home from donations! Less than 1% return to homelessness. They give all children a plaque with their name on it in their own room to give them a sense of belonging.

What we do

Work in their warehouse to check, clean and put away donated goods. Paint plaques for kids so that all children may have a plaque with their name on it. It is theirs alone. Do a home deco, going into a new space decorating it for them with Humble Designs goods.

How to help

View the schedule for upcoming events

Volunteer for 2 hours of warehouse work - sorting, cleaning, inspecting, putting away donations

Do a home deco - Go to a deco site with them, decorate a house top to bottom, arranging cabinets, the dining table, pictures, dresser tops, the kitchen bath & bedroom

Paint kids name plaques

Help trace pictures on plaques

Donate gently used, clean, no stains or tears household goods. No personal items.
