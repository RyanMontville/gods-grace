We deal with 2 charities that help the homeless:

Trials for Hope

Gives food, clothes, hope and dignity to those under the bridges, in camps and struggling homebound who may be choosing between heat, medicine and food. Those who fall through the cracks.

Humble Design

Furnishes a house top to bottom to make it a home for the homeless transitioning into housing and veterans. Gives all children a plaque with their name on it in their own room to give them a sense of belonging.
